## Patterns Worth Noting

1. **Wire loops** — Archi sometimes generates "wire_X" capabilities that just
   try to wire a previous capability, then detects that wiring as a new gap,
   ad infinitum. Fixed once but the pattern may recur.
2. **Hallucinated gap names** — Discord conversation fragments sometimes leak
   into the gap detector as capability names (e.g., "guess_couldn_read_file",
   "just_thought_possibly_spare").
3. **Rebuild loops** — Archi sometimes rebuilds capabilities it already has
   (image_analysis.py was modified 3 times in quick succession).
4. **Manual fix ratio** — roughly 1 manual fix commit per 5 Archi commits,
   suggesting the generation loop works but needs guardrails tightened.
